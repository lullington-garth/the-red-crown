Danielle listens carefully as you explain your quest and your intention of entering your brother's realm in search of Antiquity.

"'Tis a noble thing you undertake," she says when you have finished. "And one of great importance to all the magical kingdoms."

She shakes her head sadly.

"There is little I can do to aid you directly. My place is here within the Forest Between the Realms. But I can tell you of the Silver Oak, or rather the Silver Oaks, for there are two within the realm you seek."

"I do not know which one you require, and perhaps it does not matter. Both are ancient beyond measure, and both bear silver acorns. One is rooted in knowledge, the other in strength. That is all I can tell you."
