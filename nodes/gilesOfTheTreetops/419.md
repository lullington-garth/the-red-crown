You press the Sypher Stone into the hollow carved within the acorn. It is a perfect fit.

With a soft click, the stone settles into place.

At once, light begins to seep from the ground beneath the monument, flowing upward through the carved lines of the two trees. The tree upon the right glows with a silver-blue radiance, while the tree upon the left shines with a silver-green light.

Ancient magic pulses from the stone in tangible waves.

When every carved line has filled with light, the two hand-shaped depressions begin to glow with brilliant silver fire. You can feel that this ancient magic means you no harm, but it is still with great caution that you bring your hands up to the two depressions in the stone.

Through the silver-blue tree you feel purpose, wisdom and deep understanding.

From the silver-green tree you get a sense of strength, protection and quiet nurturing.

You take a deep breath and place your hands upon the stone.

The sensation is both startling and welcoming. You feel not one ancient power, but two. Though both are old beyond reckoning, each springs from a different stream of magic and carries a very different nature.

Power surges from the silver-blue tree.

Then the light gradually fades as the ancient power withdraws and the monument falls dark once more.
