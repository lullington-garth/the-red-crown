As you venture deeper into the trees, you begin to hear the steady sound of wood being chopped.

Taking care not to approach too closely, you peer through the undergrowth. In a small clearing stands a modest woodland hut. Outside, within its tiny yard, a slender red-haired woman is splitting logs.

She wears buff hide trousers, a green tunic and a leather waistcoat. Despite her slight frame, she swings the axe with remarkable strength and skill, splitting each log cleanly with a single blow.

She splits the final piece of wood, lowers the axe, wipes the sweat from her brow and reaches for a longbow resting beside the hut door.

Before you can blink, an arrow thuds into the tree beside which you are hiding.

"Come out or be gone," calls the woman. "But do not spy upon me."
