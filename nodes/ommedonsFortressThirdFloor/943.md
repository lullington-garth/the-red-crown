You search the cramped chamber.

The ledgers are filled with dense notations and work schedules, useless to you, with diagrams concerning systems you do not understand and have no time to study.

The only thing of interest you can see is a diagram singled out from the rest and rolled flat on the desk.

The diagram is titled:

"The Truth"

Underneath this someone has written the words:

"The truth is the perfect lie."

The words have been underlined and circled.

You have no idea what this means, but it was obviously important information to someone.
