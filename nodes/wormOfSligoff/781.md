Calling upon the power of the Larva Ring, you raise your hand towards the towering creature.

A pale green light radiates from the ring and washes over the worm. It looks confused, then compliant, as its aggression fades. The great beast lowers its head and emits a deep rumbling sound, almost like the purr of some gigantic animal.

Slowly it bows before you, then coils itself aside, clearing a safe path around the slime pool. Though its enormous eyes never leave you, there is no hostility within them.
