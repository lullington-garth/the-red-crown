You hurl the Orb of Dragon Fire with all your strength. The glowing sphere arcs through the storm-dark air and strikes the worm squarely upon its hide, shattering on impact.

A roaring inferno erupts from within, unleashing the fury of dragon-fire. Flames engulf the worm, racing across its body from head to tail. The creature thrashes wildly, its screams echoing across the desert, as in a desperate attempt to save itself it rolls in the acidic slime. But even the corrosive sludge cannot extinguish the magical blaze, and within moments the worm is reduced to blackened chunks of burning flesh.

The beast screams ring across the plains then die. It flops into the burning acid and is still. The path ahead lies open once more.
