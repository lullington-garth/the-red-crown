You kill dozens of sandmurks. Then dozens more. Your sword sweeps through the swarm indiscriminately, but it makes no difference.

The creatures continue their dreadful, relentless chorus. There is no peace. The sound worms its way ever deeper into your thoughts until reality itself begins to unravel.

You collapse to the ground screaming, your mind unravelling. You curl up, screaming as the chitter, chitter, chitter drives you beyond the reach of sanity.

Your quest ends here.
