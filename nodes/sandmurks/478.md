The moment the queen dies, the effect is immediate. The surviving sandmurks scatter in all directions and their maddening chittering abruptly ceases.

The sudden silence is almost as shocking as the noise itself. Your head pounds and the world spins around you. Unable to remain conscious any longer, you collapse beside the queen's corpse.

When you awaken, daylight fills the clearing. Nearby, the queen's ugly carcass lies where it fell.
