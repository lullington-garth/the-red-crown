The guardian remains silent. Slowly, he extends a weathered hand. Remembering your brother's instructions, you present the token you collected during your journey.

The giant studies it for several moments before returning it to you.

Not entirely sure what is happening, you attempt to pass the gatekeeper. He steps in front of you, staff in hand, blocking your way with effortless authority.

It is then that you realise you have brought the wrong token.

The giant raises his staff and points to one side. As he does so, a vast stone archway erupts from the earth.

Beyond it you can see the Parting Stones and, beyond them, the familiar town below.

Your heart sinks. To head through that arch would be to fail your quest, but you know there is no possibility of defeating or deceiving a gatekeeper. Such beings are chosen precisely because they cannot be swayed.

You stand in the clearing for a long while, the gatekeeper impassive, before you finally accept the truth of the matter and, with a heavy heart, lower your head and walk towards the archway.

Your quest ends here.
