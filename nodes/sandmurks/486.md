The guardian extends a weathered hand towards you. Assuming he seeks payment, you drop several gold coins into his palm.

He studies them briefly before tossing them aside.

Puzzled, you offer more.

This time he discards them immediately. Then a deep {guardianCry} rumbles from his chest and he slams the butt of his staff into the earth.

A vast stone archway materialises beside you. Through it you can see the clearing where the Parting Stones stand and the town beyond.

This time the gatekeeper's meaning is unmistakable. Whatever offering is required to pass, you do not possess it, and he will allow you no further into the realm.

You run through your options but find none. You may not continue, and you cannot defeat or deceive the ancient sentinel standing before you. They are chosen as realm guardians for that very reason.

With no other options open to you, you turn towards the archway knowing that you have failed.

Your quest ends here.
