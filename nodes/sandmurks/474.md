You raise the Flute of Healing Sleep to your lips and play a simple melody.

The music is beautiful, but utterly ineffective against the horde.

With the chittering drowning out every note, the sandmurks' cry tears into your thoughts, shredding your sanity piece by piece. You do not remember dropping the flute. You do not remember falling.

You only know that at some point you find yourself curled into a ball upon the forest floor, screaming as the relentless noise strips away your grip on reality.

Your quest ends here.
