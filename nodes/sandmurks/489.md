You fumble and find the scroll you seek. The Dark Whisper. It is a desperate solution, but this is a desperate situation.

The spell was designed as a curse, one that plunges its victim into a deep and exhausting magical slumber, trapped in a fitful sleep and unable to wake until the enchantment fades many hours later.

It will not work on the sandmurks, but it might save you from them if you inflict it upon yourself. Drawing upon every ounce of willpower you possess, you intone the spell, targeting its effects upon yourself.

Darkness claims you immediately.

The curse must have worked, as the next thing you know you awaken with bright sunlight filtering through the trees. The forest is silent save for the buzz of insects, the twittering of birds and the rustling of the wind through the canopy above.

The nocturnal sandmurks are gone.

Your sanity is intact, but you are mentally and physically drained. The curse has done its job but taken its toll.

Lose 3 STAMINA.
