You snatch the Wolf Whistle from your pack and blow hard. If it makes a sound, it is utterly drowned beneath the maddening chorus of the sandmurks.

Panic grips you. The creatures' cries are already burrowing into your thoughts, scraping at the edges of your sanity. Gritting your teeth, you raise the whistle once more and blow with all your strength.

Still nothing.

The chittering becomes unbearable. You stagger and fall to your knees, clutching your head as the sound tears through your mind.

You are barely aware as a great grey shape bursts from the darkness. A wolf.

It ploughs into the horde. Sandmurks fly through the air as he seizes them in his jaws and hurls them aside. Head lowered, he charges through their ranks like a living battering ram, snapping necks and shattering bones as he goes.

But the sandmurks seem unconcerned, and the awful burrowing chittering continues.

You scream as the noise threatens to shatter your mind completely.

The great grey wolf locates the bloated queen at the heart of the colony and tears her down. The chittering eases. With their queen lost, the sandmurk horde scatters into the darkness and, at last, the dreadful chittering fades.
