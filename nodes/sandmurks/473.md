With a strangled cry, you charge into the horde.

The sandmurks are ugly creatures with pale, sagging skin, oversized square teeth and wide, flat, almost toad-like faces. They are little larger than rabbits and fall in droves beneath your sweeping blade.

Yet even though you slay the repellent creatures in great numbers, they seem utterly unconcerned, and their maddening chittering rolls on, clawing at your sanity as you carve a path through their ranks.
