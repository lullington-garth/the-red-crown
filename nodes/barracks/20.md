You decide to enter the town by way of Crow Arch Lane. The road is alive with noise and movement long before you reach the town proper. Traders shout over one another as they raise canvas stalls beside the roadside, while the smell of roasting meat and fresh bread drifts from busy food vendors. Farmers guide overloaded carts towards the markets and labourers hurry past carrying crates, timber and sacks of grain.

You pass the toll house without incident and soon arrive at the stone walls of Blackwatch Barracks, headquarters of the town militia. The barracks are sturdy and severe, built for discipline rather than comfort. Militia guards patrol constantly, their officers known for running a tight ship, always aware of current affairs. Of late, however, there have been whispers of growing suspicion within their ranks, especially towards users of magic.
