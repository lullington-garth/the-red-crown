Beyond the door lies a circular chamber of black stone. Runes glow faintly along the walls, pulsing in time with a low, rhythmic hum. At the centre of the room stands a tall crystalline obelisk, shot through with veins of dark light. Metal bands encircle the obelisk, etched with runes of binding, measurement and flow. Four leather straps are affixed to the obelisk worryingly spaced perfectly to hold a person prostrate against the stone. 

A narrow channel runs from the base of the obelisk into the wall beyond—whatever this device draws is carried elsewhere.

You step closer and the runes flare brighter. Bringing your hand towards the stone you can feel a strong, hungry magic within. A conduit, but which way that conduit runs and what it carries you can not tell.

Will you...
