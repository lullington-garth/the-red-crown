You step into a plushly furnished chamber lit by glittering candles. Lounging on a chaise longue is an emaciated woman in a maid's outfit. 

She stands as you enter, smiling. There is a hunger her eyes. Before you know what's happening she opens her mouth...

...impossibly wide. Hanging within, swaying gently in her throat, is the small silver bell you rang at the food table.

"Ah, my darling," says the maid, somehow talking with her mouth now opened to something like the size of your head, "you rang my bell and now we are in a contract of service and supply."

The bell tinkles in her cavernous mouth as she speaks.

"You did not wait for me to render my side of the contract, that is your right..."

Tink. Tink. Tink.

She takes a step towards you, her mouth dizzyingly wide.

"...but there is still the matter of payment."

Combat doesn't even cross your mind. You feel compelled to pay this woman.
