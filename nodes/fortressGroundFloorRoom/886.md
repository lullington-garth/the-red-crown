A few moments after you ring the bell, an emaciated woman in an ill-fitting maid's outfit enters the room. She carries a silver platter from which she serves you a delicious smelling roast and pours you a goblet of rich wine.

She looks at you hungrily, then, with a tiny bow, leaves you to your meal.

It is incredible. You have rarely eaten so well and it does indeed sustain you.

You feel vitalised...

...and yet somehow empty.

With horror, you realise that you cannot feel your magic. It's not that your magical reservoir has been drained. It has gone.

The food must have been cursed. Ommadon has removed your magic, no doubt adding it to his own reserves of power.

Without magic, you will never be able to take his crown.

You will never build a kingdom to protect all magical kind.

Your quest is over.
