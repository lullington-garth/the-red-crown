You reach into your pouch and produce the Coin of Closure.

The Bell Demon freezes.

The bell in her mouth gives a sharp, discordant chime.

You place the coin on the floor between you.

It snaps to the ground as you release it—a clear, final sound that echoes through the chamber.

The maid's form begins to unravel, her flesh peeling away into shadow.

"Paid," she says, bowing her now-shadowy head.
