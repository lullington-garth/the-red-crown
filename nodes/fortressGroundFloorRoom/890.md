You search your belongings, but find nothing that feels worthy of her. You offer gold, but she merely shakes her head.

Eventually she sighs, then smiles with a muffled tinkle.

"Then we must conclude the contract another way."

She opens her mouth. The bell tolls. A thunderous sound this time.

It peals again and the demon's mouth opens still wider.

As the bell pounds a third time, all you can see is the cavernous maw and the bell, now of cathedral proportions, ringing with head-splitting peals.

The sound consumes you, thundering in your chest, burning through your mind, draining something deeper than magic—promise, purpose and possibility.

Darkness closes in as the bell rings a final time and payment is made.
