A wash of strange smells and crackling magic drifts from the open archway ahead. Beyond it lies the Enchanters Exchange — a sprawling indoor market where magic is traded as casually as fish at a dockside stall. Robed figures haggle loudly over bottled enchantments while hooded scribes scratch notes into ledgers bound with chain. Sparks burst from worktables, curses hiss from locked jars and somewhere deeper people take cover as something explodes in green flame.

You pause at the threshold of the busy market.
