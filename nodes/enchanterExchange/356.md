The Exchange is larger inside than it first appeared. Long aisles twist away beneath hanging lanterns filled with floating lights, while dozens of stalls crowd together beneath faded velvet banners. Everywhere you look there is movement — witches grinding powders beneath mortars, merchants relabelling enchantments into cheaper bottles and apprentices chasing escaped spells through the crowds with huge nets.

At one table a man carefully weighs curses upon silver scales while at another a pair of dwarfs hammer glowing runes into the blade of a screaming sword. Everywhere the air crackles with magic.
