You sit close to the pair of elderly wizards who are hunched over a table covered in sketches, maps and parchments. They are speaking excitedly about the Parting Stones.

“They’ve been sighted,” says one of them. “Imagine being able to cross over into one of the core realms.” He hiccups with excitement then notices your interest.

“They are close,” he says. "They really are." He pushes a map of magical flowlines towards you. The lines move constantly on the page, but from what you can see the stones are indeed very close. Good news indeed.

“You’ll never find them though,” says the other man lighting up a long, curled pipe. “They say only the brothers and non-magic folk can see them. Ahh but to travel over…” he says wistfully.
