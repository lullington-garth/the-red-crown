You kneel and place the acorn in the pool. As the seed touches the surface silver fire bursts from the water, throwing you backwards. It roars upward in a blinding column that tears through the ghostly branches above. The chamber trembles under its force as roots strain and stone groans.

“Ommadon…”  a vast, deep, dominant voice thunders around the room, "seeks to sap my power. To divide the realms, to part that which must remain whole.”

The silver fire parts to reveal the pool's surface where glimpses—fractured and fleeting—of another place, dark and twisted can be seen.

“Within his keep lies a sister pool to this one. Hidden and bound. It holds the strength to call forth an aid from the distant future.” The light dims, just slightly. “Ommadon has corrupted my sacred pool, veiled it in deception. Fractured the future it foretells.” The light dims further. “You must choose… wisely. Not all futures are favoured towards you.”

The silver light dies and the fire fades. Leaving the pool still once more and the chamber in ringing silence.

You feel something in you hand. The silver acorn has been returned to you.

"Choose wisely..." The deep voice now nothing more than a whisper on the breeze.
