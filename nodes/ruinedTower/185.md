With the bathhouse now behind you, you continue along Brook Meadow Path. The road winds gently through quieter land, where the sounds of the settlement fade beneath birdsong and the rustling of tall grass. Passing an old temple and a cluster of weathered farm buildings, you soon notice a ruined stone tower standing upon a low rise ahead.

Though age has worn away much of its upper structure, colourful banners hang from the walls and smoke rises from braziers near the entrances. From a distance you can already hear laughter, the clatter of wooden weapons and the distant sound of drums. Two separate entrances lead inside the tower grounds.
