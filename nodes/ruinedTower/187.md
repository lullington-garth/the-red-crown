An elderly doorman wearing a faded crimson cloak greets you as you approach the entrance. He leans comfortably upon a spear.

‘Ah, a visitor! Excellent timing,’ he says. ‘The tower offers two experiences. Both cost 1 Gold Piece, though they differ rather greatly in mood.’

He gestures towards two separate grilled stairways disappearing into the ruins.

‘Door one is a historical tour, full of performances and tales of Tanglemoore Keep. Door two... well, that one should be closed to the public in my opinion. Fair warning it’s haunted. Some types like a fright and you might be one of them. You go down at your own risk though.’
