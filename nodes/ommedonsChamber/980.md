In time, the council of brothers, now only three, gathers once more with solemn purpose. The immense power held within the crown, not tamed but no longer wild or covetous, is released and merged with those other magics of the deep and the stars, of peace and tranquillity, of nature and enchantment.

Together, the three remaining brothers shape a kingdom — a sanctuary for all magical folk. A land apart from man, but not closed to him. Man will still access the kingdom through a flash of inspiration, through a spark of imagination, or on the back of a dream.

The sanctity of magic will be preserved through the years... the centuries... the ages. A part of man for all time. And whenever man might call upon magic, it will be there for him.
