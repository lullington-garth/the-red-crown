You stand before the ancient pool.

Before Ommadon can throw another obstacle at you, you draw the Silver Acorn from your pack and drop it into the water. It strikes the still surface with barely a ripple.

For a moment, nothing happens. Then the pool begins to stir.

Ripples spread across its surface, threaded with sparkling starlike light. The blue veins surrounding the pool flare brighter, pulsing with a slow, steady rhythm. The roots of Antiquity hum with power, their light flowing back toward the water like sap returning to a living tree.

You feel it now—the weight of every choice you have made, gathering toward this single moment. Without thinking, you step closer. The water darkens. Shapes swirl beneath the surface, ghostlike at first, then solidifying. Something is approaching.

Mist rolls across the pool and a figure begins to emerge. A man climbs from the water and steps onto the stone beside it. The roots of Antiquity blaze with blue light and then slowly settle.

Peter stands before you.

Ommadon laughs. A deep, rolling sound that shakes the chamber.

"So this is your champion? You pit this puny mortal against the full might of Ommadon?"

Peter, unperturbed, steps forward, calm and resolute, ready to face the master of dark magic.
