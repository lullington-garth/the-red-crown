You step onto the shifting lattice of runes. The symbols flare and lock briefly beneath your feet. You seep your magic into them and the collapsing path stabilises just long enough for you to advance.

Blue lightning coils around your arms, drawn to the magic you are calling to traverse the runic path, while all the time below you, the lava boils.

Heat bites through armour, your breath scorching your lungs.

You move quickly, reinforcing glyphs as they form, rewriting others as they dissolve. The path responds to your will—not fully, you can feel it resisting you —but enough.

Ommadon watches, amused.

"Yes," he says softly. "Spend it. Spend it all, brother."

The symbols brighten and power floods through you. For a moment you feel stronger than you have at any point in your quest.
