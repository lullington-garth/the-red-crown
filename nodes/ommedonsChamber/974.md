You pause before stepping onto the path.

You take a deep, steadying breath, assessing the stones before you. You notice that oddly, they appear to have fractured fairly evenly. You look closer taking in every detail, the tiny chip, the hairline crack, the shimmer of heat, the fire licking through the broken surface, and then you see it - a pattern.

Strangely which there are at least 100 fractured stones ahead of you , they appear to repeat in design, an of the various patterns of stone there is only one that traverses the full width of the fissure. It seen the floor itself is guiding you, if only you pay attention.

"Choose already!" growls Ommadon, but you will not be hurried.

You gingerly apply your weight to a stone on what you believe to be the safe path. It holds. To prove your theory you tap a stone of a differing pattern with your staff. It shatters and tumbles into the fire below. You chose the next closest stone that matches yours. It holds... then the next...

Almost without noticing, you find yourself at the final stone. You step onto solid ground. The fractured stones shimmer once, then settle into solidity as the fissure closes over.

You are through, unscathed.
