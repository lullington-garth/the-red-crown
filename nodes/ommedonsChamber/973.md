You approach the path of shattered stone. Jagged peaks rise from the bubbling fire below, sheer, sharp and blisteringly hot to touch.

You move quickly, stone crumbling beneath you as you climb, forging a path through the broken peaks through sheer will alone. You move without hesitation, from handhold to foothold with determined confidence, the gap between you and the throne closing quickly.

You are going to make it. Just one more rise...

The ground shakes as the crumbling spine you're clinging to shatters.

Ommadon leans forward on his throne, interest flickering across his vast, inhuman features.

"Wrong," he says softly.

The broken stone slides toward the fissure taking you with it. You leap to another peak but that too snaps and tumbles throwing you into free fall. As you plummet you grab at passing outcrops, but it is no use, the whole fissure wall is in collapse.

The last thing you hear as you fall into the fissure below is Ommadon's cold, cackling laugh.
