You barely have time to recover before Ommadon rises from his throne. He lifts his staff and brings it down once, hard, upon the stone before him.

The ground splits open with a shriek of tearing stone. The violent tremor that follows throws you off balance as deep fissures rip apart the chamber floor. The tears spit fire, the heat of which you can feel before even approaching.

As the tremors subside you survey the chamber. Three distinct paths now lie before you.

To your left, the stone has buckled and churned into a brutal, uneven rise of shattered stone and fire. The path is steep, broken and unstable—but direct.

Straight ahead, the fissure has opened into a churning chasm of boiling lava over which a path of arcane symbols drift and collapse, forming an ever-changing pathway. Blue lightning crawls through the haze of shimmering symbols.

To your right, the floor remains mostly intact. The stone here appears to have shattered in a repeating pattern, fractures branching at precise angles and forming narrow stepping stones.

Which path will you take?
