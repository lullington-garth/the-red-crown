You call on your magic. Digging deep, you conjure a raging ball of rolling flame and hurl it at the spinning wall of ice...

Except it is not ice.

Too late, you notice that the pool remains calm and still beneath the wild, swirling barrier and realise that the wall is a construct of magic, placed there by Ommadon to keep an enemy at bay — an enemy whose power lies in magic.

The fireball thunders into the barrier, its magical energy adding power to the ward built to protect against such attacks.

Orange and red mix with white and blue as the two magics collide, entwine and amplify.

The wall explodes outwards.

Its power, charged by your own magic, expands in all directions. Within seconds you are surrounded by a hurricane of fire and ice.

You are consumed by the howling barrier.

Consumed by a power you came to protect.

Your quest is over.
