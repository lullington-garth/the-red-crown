The innkeeper crashes heavily to the saw-dusted floor. All eyes are on you. You are weighing up your options when one of the thugs quarrelling with the knight uses the distraction to stab him. There is an explosion of fists fury and abuse from all sides as the knight draws his sword and advances on the thugs. 

You are about to intervene, when a thin nervous-looking man tugs you over to him. You ready for a fight but the man raises his arms and says quickly, ‘I know where the Parting Stones are. I saw them. Yesterday. Past the cursed shrine south of the river.' 

There is a scream behind you, you spin around but it is just the knight taking out his last attacker. That didn't last long you think. When you turn back to the man to thank him, he is gone.
