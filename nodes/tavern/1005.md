The biggest of the men looks up at you and grins unpleasantly.

'Sure,' he rumbles. 'But this game is over. We're playin' a new one now.' He jerks a thumb towards a short ugly brute beside him. 'Pug, hold him.'

Before you can react the squat man seizes you from behind and wrenches one of your arms painfully up your back.

'So, here's the game,' says the leader with a sneer, he gestures towards the five other men gathered around the table, 'each of us gets a turn throwing a dagger at you. You’ve got one free hand to catch it with. You catch it,' he shrugs, 'fair enough. Miss it…' his grins, 'and if you survive, you pay the thrower one gold piece, if you don't survive we rob your corpse.'

The men roar with laughter.

You glare at the huge man while Pug twists your arm harder.

“What if I win?” you ask through gritted teeth.

The man barks out a laugh. ‘Bold,’ he says. ‘I can respect that. I’m an honourable man,’ all evidence points to the contrary you think, but you do not correct him. ‘If you catch all five, Pug here will give you his chainmail.’

‘Wh..What?’ stammers Pug.
