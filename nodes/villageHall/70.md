You head down Crow Arch Lane past the old weigh house until you come upon a pretty village hall standing slightly back from the street behind a low iron fence. Warm lantern light spills from its tall windows and colourful banners hang from the wooden beams beneath the roof.

A painted sign near the entrance reads:

PRINCESS MELISANDE
THE FUTURE OF THE FOUR KINGDOMS
TODAY ONLY

Standing by the open doors is a stern-looking man wearing the badge of the local militia. As you approach he holds up a hand to stop you.

‘One Gold Piece entry,’ he says. ‘And no talking during the presentation.’ He gestures seriously towards the hall behind him. ‘The Princess will be performing highly complex magic during her speech. She requires absolute concentration.’
