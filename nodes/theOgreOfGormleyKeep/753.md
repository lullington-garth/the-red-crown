You raise the horn to your lips and blow hard. The deep, resonating sound it emits echoes across the keep, the skies, and the land below. A streak of gold rips through the stormy sky and, with a roar that can only be a battle cry, the great ancient dragon Smrgol bursts from the clouds.

Fire trails his path as he streaks through the air like a comet aimed directly at the ogre. His speed is astonishing, and his roar shakes the very stone upon which you stand.

The ogre watches the dragon. If there is any indication of concern, it is shown by a slight change in his stance as he braces to face the speeding dragon.

They meet with explosive force. The ogre grunts as his wooden leg, slipping on the damp stone, gives way, and he and the raging dragon tumble across the stones in a storm of claws, teeth, and flailing mace.
