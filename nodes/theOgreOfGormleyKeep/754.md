The huge figure of the Ogre of Gormley Keep looms before you. He is every bit as fearsome as the tales claim. He stands as tall as a fully grown dragon, his broad shoulders draped in filthy furs. Two small yellow eyes glare at you where three once were. One leg ends at the knee, replaced by a crude wooden peg-leg that thumps heavily against the stone.

In one massive hand he carries a mace. The weapon alone is taller than you are.

The ogre stands impassive, his grey-green skin sickly against the storm-lit sky. His wide mouth is stretched in a grin, baring broken tusk-like teeth.

His remaining eyes stay fixed on you, but for the moment he appears content to let you make the first move.
