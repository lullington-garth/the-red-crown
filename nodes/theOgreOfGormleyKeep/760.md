You dive to the side as the ogre's mace splinters the stone where, seconds before, you stood.

There is no way you are going to beat Ommadon's ancient and magical servant.

You scan around, desperate for a way out as the ogre raises his mace again. Perhaps you can make it back to the tunnel?

You make a dash for it. Caught unawares, the ogre lurches after you. He swings his gigantic weapon in a wide arc, but he is off balance. His wooden leg slips on the wet stone and his body, with the momentum of a planetary object, is hurled inexorably forwards. With a look of wild astonishment in both your eyes and his, the Ogre of Gormley Keep tumbles over the lip of the wall.

You watch in shock as the beast punches through the cloud cover and hurtles towards the ground far below. It is too far down for you to see the crater that the falling ogre must have caused, or for the sound of the impact to reach you, but you are certain that even the Ogre of Gormley Keep could not have survived a fall of that magnitude.
