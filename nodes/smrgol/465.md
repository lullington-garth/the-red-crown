The dragon watches silently as you kneel beside it and call forth a healing spell. The magic flows from your hands and vanishes into the creature's thick scales.

The dragons chuckles. "A worthy attempt," he rasps, "but my old hide it too think for human magic to help. I appreciate the effort though."

The dragon gives you a faint nod still studying you.

"My name is Smrgol, and don't you worry about my hide. This old soldier has been in worse scrapes than this. A little rest and my magic will heal most of these scrapes."

You look a little surprised and the dragon chuckles again. "Don't think this old boy will heal, or didn't know dragons have magic? Well we don't tell everyone."

You talk with the dragon at some length and eventually conversation turns to your quest. Smrgol listens with great interest. As you finish the old dragon nods approvingly. "It's the only way," he says. "The kingdoms have been in need of unity for a longs time."

He raises a talon and a horn materialises in front of you.

"Your quest is import to me," he says, "and you showed me a kindness. Should you ever find you against an impossible foe, just blow the horn. I will come."
