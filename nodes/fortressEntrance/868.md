As you enter the fortress, you notice a small ornate door standing slightly ajar in one wall. Oddly, something about it feels familiar. Curious, you step inside.

Beyond lies a small circular chamber thick with dust. Shelves line the walls, crowded with broken furniture, cracked bottles and forgotten clutter. It appears to be little more than a storeroom, abandoned long ago.

You are about to leave when a cabinet near the back of the room catches your eye.

Partially concealed beneath a velvet cloth, it contains four objects:

A crystalline quill
A small alchemical flask
An onyx wand
A leaf-shaped amulet

With a pang of shame, you recognise them. These were gifts exchanged in the earliest days of magic, when the Four Pillars stood united. Symbols of trust, balance and alliance between the houses. You have similar relics gathering dust in a forgotten room of your own.

For a moment you find yourself wondering when friendship became rivalry, and rivalry became war.

If you can claim Ommadon's crown, perhaps some measure of balance can yet be restored.

It feels wrong to leave these tokens forgotten and collecting dust.
