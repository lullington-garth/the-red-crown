You take a swing at the serpent's head, but even with all your weight behind it your blade merely glances off the magical wood. It does not leave so much as a scratch.

"Rude," says the serpent.

Its eyes blaze bright green and the multitude of vipers surrounding it raise their heads as one, writhing and hissing in agitation. The smaller snakes spit poison and you are forced to retreat.

Not far enough it seems as the huge serpent lashes out striking with incredible speed.

You dive, avoiding its dripping fangs by a hair's breadth. You land hard against a sandy mound to the sickening sound of cracking bones... not yours, thank the gods.

Beneath the mound, hidden by years of gathering sand, lies the corpse of a long-dead adventurer. Nothing but bones now. Some other poor fool who tried to enter Ommadon's fortress uninvited.

The serpent snaps again and you scramble back, disturbing the skeleton further. As you do, you notice that not everything has perished. The skeletal hand clutches a small bird figurine. Beside that lies a patterned stone, and around its neck hangs a knotted charm.

It's a long shot, but if this adventurer was trying to pass the gates, perhaps one of these items is the key to safe passage.
