You pull the pendant from your backpack and slip it over your neck. As you do, the dull green eyes of the serpents fade to grey.

There is an almost inaudible hiss, whether from the nest of snakes or from the air that escapes as the doors gently part, you cannot tell.

They only open a crack and are far too large for you to force any wider. But the gap while tight is just about manageable.
