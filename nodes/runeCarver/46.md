The interior of the shop is warm and smells faintly of cedarwood. Shelves line every wall, crowded with polished rune stones, tiny carved idols, and jars of coloured powders. Standing behind a curved oak counter is a slander Elven woman. She smiles with an easy grace as you enter.

‘I am Selura,’ she says. ‘Welcome to my shop. Please take a look around. All stones are 2 gold pieces each. I also offer medative courses if you are interested.’

Among the myriad of stones, you notice a rolled parchment labelled as a rune spell scroll. 

‘Ahh, that came to me by accident,’ says Selura. ‘Spells are not really my field. I’m told it is good, but without knowing for sure I wouldn’t want to ask any more than 1 gold piece for it.’

As you put the scroll back down you notice a black velvet cushion upon which rests a smooth shard of dark crystal covered in faintly glowing ancient runes. It resonates with a deep magic.

‘That,’ says Selura following your gaze, ‘is not for sale.’
