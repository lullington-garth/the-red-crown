'Please, follow me,' says Selura.

She leads you through the shop to a small room at the back of the building. It is simply furnished: a woven mat covers the floor, while a single candle burns in one corner, filling the room with the faint scent of lavender.

'This is where I teach the art of True Focus,' she explains. 'It is a discipline of the mind. By learning to quiet your thoughts, you can shut out distraction, fear and confusion, allowing you to concentrate on what truly matters.'

She invites you to sit opposite her. She instructs you to close your eyes and slow your breathing letting your mind clear of all distractions.

At first your mind refuses to cooperate. Every distant sound draws your attention and your thoughts wander endlessly but each time this happens Selura guides you back to a state of True Focus.

At the end of the hour, you cannot claim to have mastered the technique, but you have gained a sound understanding of its basic principles. You thank Selura for her time and ask what you owe her.

She smiles warmly.

'Nothing at all. Meditation is a gift that should be shared freely. If it helps even one traveller find a little peace, then my reward is already more than enough.'
