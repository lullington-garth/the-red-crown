The shop door opens and Selura turns her attention to the new customer. As she does, you quietly reach towards the dark crystal shard.

As your hand enters the air around it, rune symbols flare into life across the length of the counter.

A violent shock throws you hard onto the polished wooden floor and before you fully know what’s happening you find a smoke sentinel kneeling on your chest. You feel it draining you.

LOSE 1 SKILL

Selura spins round. ‘That’s my security shard,’ she sighs.

Before you can speak, the sentinel seizes hold of your robes, drags you up and hurls you bodily into the street. The door slams shut behind you with enough force to rattle the windows.

You decide it would be wise not to return.
