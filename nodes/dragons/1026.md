You choose to ignore all the distractions and head deeper into {playerStats.visitingBrotherName} realm. As you turn your back on the other paths, a low rumble rolls across the skies and the ground quakes. Once again, you are reminded of the sheer willpower that your brother is exerting to hold the realm, and you recall his warning to tread the paths that best respect the nature of his realm.

As the ground settles the low rumble of  the sky is replaced by heavy wingbeats. You stop to look up and are alarmed to see a large flock of dragons streaking across the sky with some purpose. Thankfully, they appear to have no interest in you, but you stay very still all the same, unwilling to draw attention to yourself until they have passed.
