You place a single Gold Piece into the wizard's waiting hand. With a satisfied nod, he traces a circle in the air with the tip of his staff, murmuring a handful of unfamiliar words. Threads of pale blue light spiral around your feet before rising to envelop you entirely. For a fleeting instant, the world dissolves into shimmering light, and you experience the curious sensation of standing nowhere at all.

Just as suddenly, the light fades. Solid ground returns beneath your boots, the sounds and smells of Brookdown Crossing come rushing back around you as you find yourself standing in Martket Street. You turn to thank the wizard, but of course he is not there.
