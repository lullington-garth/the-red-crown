You could easily spend an entire day exploring the contents of a shop like this. Every shelf and cabinet seems to contain something strange or valuable from some distant corner of the world.

Several objects catch your attention amongst the countless curiosities, among which are a couple of interesting looking scrolls and, locked behind a glass display, a curious gold coin bearing the label:

COIN OF SINGLE PURCHASE

‘Strange little thing that,’ says the pawnbroker when you ask about it. ‘No idea how it ended up in my possession. That coin allows its owner to purchase any single item regardless of cost. Why would you pawn an item like that I ask you? Only works once, mind you, but even so...’

He opens the case and places the coin on the counter.

‘Far too valuable for me to put a price upon it. However, I would be willing to exchange it for another magical item of equal interest.’
