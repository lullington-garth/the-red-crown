You uncork the potion and gulp down the enhanced potion; Veil of the Unseen granting you invisibility and Ranger's Bane ensuring you leave no tracks.

The effects are instant and remarkable. You doubt that the dragons could detect you if you were riding on the back of one of them. Even so you choose to move with caution across the wasteland.

You need not have worried, though. With the enhanced potion concealing not only your body, but your tracks too, the dragons above don't even break a beat, it is as though you do not exist.

The great beasts sweep overhead without a second glance.
