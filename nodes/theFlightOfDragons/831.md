You lower your head and sprint across the open plain. For a few precious moments you dare to believe you might succeed. Then a thunderous roar echoes overhead.

The dragons have seen you. Of course they have. There is nowhere to hide upon the open sands and no man alive can outrun a hunting dragon let alone thousands of them.

Fire erupts around you. The ground explodes beneath your feet, as dragons descend in droves from the storm clouds. There is no time to curse your foolishness.

Your quest for the Red Crown ends here.
