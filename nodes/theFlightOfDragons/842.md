You hold the Sky Binder Chain high above your head and with a sharp tug snap it in half to force the dragons to ground.

The magic is surprisingly strong. Within seconds, the dragons start to descend. You feel a moment of smug satisfaction as your magic proves stronger than that of a horde of dragons.

Then, like a slap in the face, reality catches up with you. The chain may well force dragons from the sky, but it does absolutely nothing else.

You have little time to dwell on the wisdom of calling attention to a horde of dragons that you know are hunting you with ill intentions and then forcing them to the very ground you are standing on.

Within moments dozens of the enormous beasts you have forced to land are all around you.

Smoke curls from their nostrils. Their burning eyes fix upon you.

You try a friendly wave but...

Your quest for the Red Crown is over.
