Making sure that the ring is secure on your finger and that the magic is still strong you take a step forward. You check the ground you have just left; there are no marks, no sign that you were ever there. The ring is masking your movements perfectly. That, along with your being invisible to the horde above gives you the confidence to start moving across the open plain.

To your great relief the dragons pay you no attention at all. They swoop over the ground but treat it with the indifference they would any other dry, empty stretch of endless sand.
