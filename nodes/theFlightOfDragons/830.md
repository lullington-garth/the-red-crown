The devil is a distant memory when between forks of scarlet lightning flickering within the rumbling depths of the strom torn sky you notice something. A smudge of sorts.

You watch it as to travel, and you are disquitened to notice that it is slowly, but steadily growing. The further you march deeper and deeper into Ommadon's dark domain the faster it grows finally resolving into... you are not sure what. Distance is hard to judge. You can't tell if it's an insect swarm not far off or maybe birds further away. It is expanding at an alarming rate though, the smudge now dragged wide across the shimmering horizon.

You keep watch with a sense of dread rising inside you, until, with a sinking sense of horror, the horde is finally close enough to make out...

A flight of dragons. Thousands of them sweeping across the storm torn sky, and if the rumours are true, all of them under the power of Ommadon, and most likely they are searching for you.
