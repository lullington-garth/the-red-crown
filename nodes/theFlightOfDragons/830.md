You continue your march. The red wasteland stretches endlessly before you, broken occasionally by jagged rocks protruding from the crimson sands like broken teeth. Overhead, dark storm clouds churn across the sky, trembling to contain pent-up thunder. Forks of scarlet lightning flicker within their rumbling depths, illuminating the wasteland in brief flashes of blood-red light.

You have been watching a smudge on the horizon which has been steadily growing as you march deeper and deeper into Ommadon's dark domain. It is now growing quickly, resolving into... you are not sure what. Distance is hard to judge. You can't tell if it's an insect swarm not far off or maybe birds further away. It is expanding at an alarming rate though, the smudge now dragged wide across the shimmering horizon.

You keep watch with a sense of dread rising inside you, until, with a sinking sense of horror, the horde is finally close enough to make out...

A flight of dragons. Thousands of them sweeping across the storm torn sky, and if the rumours are true, all of them under the power of Ommadon, and most likely they are searching for you.
