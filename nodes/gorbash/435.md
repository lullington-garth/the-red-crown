Ahead, an enormous dragon squats before the entrance of a cave system, lazily biting chunks from the surrounding limestone and swallowing then whole. Its scales are deep emerald green and its broad snout is crowned with a pair of ivory horns. Golden scales cover its throat, chest and belly, glittering whenever they catch the light. 

When he's finished eating the cave front he thrusts its head into the cave mouth and bellows. "Diamonds! Bring me diamonds!"

When nothing happens he looks a little bemused and mutters, "It always works for the old man."

He tries snorting a stream of fire into the cave instead and this seems to have the desired effect for a moment latter panicked dwarves come rushing out carrying sacks overflowing with glittering gems.

Will you distract the dragon so the dwarves can escape, help the dwarves some other way, attempt to strike a bargain with the dragon, or leave them to their fate?
