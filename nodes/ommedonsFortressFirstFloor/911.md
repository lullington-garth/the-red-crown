Your reflection smiles, stepping out of the sphere.

As its foot touches the chamber floor, your vision blurs, your stomach lurches and you find yourself surrounded by glass. You are trapped inside the sphere, watching stunned as your reflection, now solid, stretches, looks back at you and smiles. All you can do is hammer on the sphere, your screams trapped by glass, as your reflection turns and heads for the door, leaving you imprisoned in its place for eternity.

Your quest is over.
