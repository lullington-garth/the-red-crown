"Interesting," says the man. He scratches his beard. "You ask not how to seize power, but instead for the knowledge to aid your path."

"You will need Antiquity's silver acorn to access the Dark Lord's inner chambers. An irony, if you ask me. Protecting oneself with the very item that can be your undoing. But who am I to fathom Ommadon's black mind?"
