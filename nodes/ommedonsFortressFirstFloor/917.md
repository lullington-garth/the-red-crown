The man finishes scribing and turns to face you. His eyes are sharp but weary.

"I am the Custodian of Measures," he says. "I maintain the concordances, thresholds and symbolic keys of this fortress, and there are an awful lot of them, so do be quick."

He taps his slate.

"You're obviously one of the brothers, no doubt here for the crown, so tell me, what do you seek? Understanding, or advantage?"
