"Advantage."

"I see," says the Custodian. "Then you misunderstand your place here. Be gone. I have much to do."

He turn his back on you placing the slate in his hand down onto the desk once more. He picks up his chalk and continue his writing.

"You will need a beast beyond my measure should you wish to batter down his door." he says, almost to himself.

You will obviously garner nothing further from this strange man.
