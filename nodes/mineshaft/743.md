As the guardian collapses, the chamber falls silent.

The crimson glow slowly fades first from the guardian and then from the surrounding crystals until only the light of your lamp remains.

With the light of the crystals gone you now notice the walls are covered with large rose like flowers. You hold the lamp close to them. Their petals are a deep scarlet, almost the colour of fresh blood, and faint wisps of golden pollen drift from their centres. The scent they release is strangely intoxicating—beautiful, yet somehow unsettling.
