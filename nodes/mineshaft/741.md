The tunnel finally opens into a vast chamber bathed in a dull crimson glow.

Crystals protrude from every surface. Strange veins of ruby-coloured light pulse through them, spreading across the walls like roots beneath flesh.

At the centre of the cavern stands a towering humanoid figure seemingly formed from crystal and stone. You are just wondering if it is some kind of statute, or an idol maybe when its eyes flare red and with a grinding roar, it tears itself free from the rock and advances towards you.
