You have not travelled far into the dustly lands of Ommadon's red realm when you discover the entrance to a long-abandoned mine shaft. Thick wooden planks have been nailed across the opening and the word UNSAFE has been scrawled across them in sand blasted red paint.

Through the narrow gaps between the boards you glimpse darkness and the shadowy outline of old support beams. They groan softly under the weight of the earth above. A stream of icy air slips through the cracks and brushes across your face.
