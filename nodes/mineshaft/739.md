The central tunnel descends deeper into the mine. The air grows steadily warmer and carries a sharp acidic tang that stings your nostrils and burns at your lungs.

As you head deeper you start to notice a strange substance coating the walls. Thick translucent veins pulse beneath its surface as though blood flows through a living creature buried within the stone itself. Against all common sense, curiosity compels you to touch it. 

Spark leap across your hand sending a burning sensation racing up your arm. You decide not to do that again.

Nursing your stinging hand you reach a point where the tunnel divides. To one side you spot a narrow crack in the rock. It looks painfully tight, but you believe you could force your way through.
