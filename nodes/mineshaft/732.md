The stinging smell of acid fades as another odour overwhelms it—the hot stench of decay and death.

Covering your mouth, you step into a low chamber littered with bones. While most are animal remains, there are others, which are unmistakably human. Creatures, mostly rats you notice, lay in various stages of decomposition scattered across the floor.

You are about to move on when a deep tremor shakes the cavern.

The loose debris begins to slide and dance across the ground. With a deafening crack, the earth splits open before you and a gigantic maggot-skinned creature erupts from below. Thick ropes of saliva drip from its mandibled maw, its hundreds of legs clicking wildly as it fixes you with hungry black eyes.
