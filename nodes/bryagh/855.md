With a final cry of pain and fury, Bryagh collapses heavily upon the blood-red stones before the fortress gates. The ground trembles beneath the weight of the dying dragon. Smoke drifts slowly from his nostrils and his great yellow eyes begin to lose their terrible fire.

You stand motionless, scarcely believing that the monstrous guardian of Ommadon has been slain.

The huge wooden doors of the fortress now stand unguarded before you. Beyond them lies the unknown horrors waiting within Ommadon’s stronghold.

It is as you gather your strength and make to step past the enormous body of the fallen dragon, that you notice your horse. It lays slashed and panting a victim of the battle, its life force spilling out onto the scorched red sand. You kneel beside the beast, stroking its mane, doing your best to comfort it in its final moments. It brings you great sadness as the horse slips into death, its ragged breathing now still. It also presents you with a problem. Your horse was carrying a lot of your equipment. You must decide what items are essential to carry going forwards.
