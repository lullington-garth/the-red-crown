You reach into your backpack and scatter the flowers you collected at the mineshaft upon the dusty read sand between yourself and Bryagh. The strange red petals whirl in the hot air and release a sharp, sweet scent. The effect upon the dragon is immediate.

Bryagh recoils, his yellow eyes widening. Smoke pours from his nostrils as his massive head droops. One of his great forelegs buckles beneath him and for a moment it seems like the mighty guardian might fall. You have dropped enough Dragon Rose to knock out any ordinary dragon. But Bryagh is no ordinary dragon.

With an enraged snarl he shakes himself like a beast shedding water, scattering loose scales across the sandy ground. His wings spread wide and he steadies himself once more before unleashing a deafening roar that echoes across the mountains.
