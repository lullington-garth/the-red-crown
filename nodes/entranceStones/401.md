As you pass beneath the ancient archway, you are struck by a sense of teeming life, abundant nature, and gentle enchantment. A soft breeze brings with it the scents of fertile earth, forest, and brook.

A shimmer of emerald light gathers before you, rippling the air like the surface of a disturbed pond, and as you watch, a familiar figure takes shape: Carolinus.
The astral projection, though insubstantial, is unmistakably him. His green robes billow around him, and his wise eyes regard you with quiet approval.
"Welcome, brother," he says, his voice echoing across the realms. "You have taken your first steps upon the Proving Path. May fortune favour you, and may your courage not fail."
The spectral wizard raises a hand in blessing.

"The road ahead is not without peril. Before you may enter my realm, you must pass through the lands between worlds and earn the favour of the Gatekeeper. Remember this well: proper payment must be offered, or your journey will end before it has truly begun."
The image begins to fade.

"Go now. I shall await your arrival. Oh, and if you see that pup Gorbash, please ask him to stop eating the cows. I need their milk."
