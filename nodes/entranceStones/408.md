As you pass beneath the ancient archway, you are struck by a sense of vastness, as though you stand beneath an endless sky while gazing into the deepest ocean. A cool breeze washes over you, carrying with it the scent of salt spray, distant storms, and crisp mountain air.

A shimmer of blue light gathers before you, rippling the air like moonlight dancing upon a midnight sea, and as you watch, a familiar figure takes shape: Solarius.

The astral projection, though insubstantial, is unmistakably him. His robes of deep blue billow around him like a star-filled night sky, while silver runes glimmer along their hems. His keen eyes shine with the light of distant constellations as they regard you with quiet approval.

"Welcome, brother," he says, his voice echoing across the realms. "You have taken your first steps upon the Proving Path. May fortune favour you, and may your courage not fail."

The spectral wizard raises a hand in blessing.

"The road ahead is not without peril. Before you may enter my realm, you must pass through the lands between worlds and earn the favour of the Gatekeeper. Remember this well: proper payment must be offered, or your journey will end before it has truly begun."

The image begins to fade.

"Go now. I shall await your arrival. Oh, and if you meet Smrgol, please warn him to be careful. I hear Ommadon is recruiting dragons to his cause."
