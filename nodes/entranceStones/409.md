As you pass beneath the ancient archway, a profound sense of peace washes over you. A gentle breeze brushes against your skin, carrying with it the scents of clean air, distant rain, and flowering blossoms borne upon the wind.

A shimmer of golden light gathers before you, swirling through the air like wisps of cloud caught in an unseen current, and as you watch, a familiar figure takes shape: Lo Tae Zhao.

The astral projection, though insubstantial, is unmistakably him. His flowing robes of white and gold drift around him suspended by the winds of his realm. His calm eyes regard you with quiet wisdom and inner peace.

"Welcome, brother," he says, his voice echoing across the realms. "You have taken your first steps upon the Proving Path. May fortune favour you, and may your courage not fail."

The spectral wizard raises a hand in blessing.

"The road ahead is not without peril. Before you may enter my realm, you must pass through the lands between worlds and earn the favour of the Gatekeeper. Remember this well: proper payment must be offered, or your journey will end before it has truly begun."

The image begins to fade.

"Go now. I shall await your arrival. I hear Danielle and Giles of the Tree Tops are currently visiting the Forest Between Realms. Please pass on my blessings should you encounter them."
