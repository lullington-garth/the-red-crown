You draw out the Flute of Healing Sleep and place it to your lips.

You play a bright gentle melody, one you have always liked. The notes drifts across the wasteland. You let them wash over you, filling your mind and reminding you of happier days. For a moment the crushing darkness recedes.

You chance a glance at the immense sky blotting black hand above, and the music falters. You try and pick up the tune again, but the notes come out melancholy. Soon the tune is a sad lament, despair seeping through the melody like poison bleeding into water.

A wave of despair brings you to your knees. You drop the flute all hop lost as you curl up and let the red sands wash over your body.

Your quest for the red crown has failed.
