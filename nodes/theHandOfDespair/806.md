You raise the Shield of Saturn toward the darkening sky. The deep etched runes blaze across its surface.

A golden barrier forms around you as beam of brilliant light erupts upward tearing a hole through the black hand. It recoils in the sky fingers unravelling into smoke, the black plumes dissolving under the ferocity of the shield's light. As it does you can feel the pervasive black magic lift from your mind and flee back to its maker.

A thunderous cry echoes across the wasteland. Then the darkness is gone. The red storm lit sky returns, unpleasant, but still a blessed relief after the cloud of dark magic.
