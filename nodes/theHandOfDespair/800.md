The barren red wastes stretch endlessly before you. Heat shimmers across the horizon and the wind whispers through drifts of crimson sand.

It takes you a moment to notice the shift in the light, the slight nudge towards a darker sky.

You look up and your blood runs cold.

Amongst the storm lit sky another cloud is forming. Vast, black and unnatural, it twists like the choking smoke of a wildfire as it slowly assumes form. Huge sky spanning fingers unfurl from the smoke cloud as a gigantic hand consumes the sky above you.

As you stare, a terrible pressure settles upon your mind. Dark thoughts seep into your consciousness. Every hardship, every failure and every fear rises unbidden to the surface. The fortress ahead seems impossibly distant and your quest hopeless.
