You pull out The Stoneheart Glyph, it grows warm against your skin.

A pulse of ancient power surges through it and a barrier of grey light forms around your mind blocking the worst of Ommadon's dark magic. 

But not all of it. Hairline cracks spread through the glyph as the Hand of Despair presses relentlessly against its protection. The rune can shield you only for so long. Already dark thoughts are beginning to creep through the gaps.

Roll one die to determine how many rounds you must endure the Hand of Despair.

At the start of each round, make a Skill Check.

This will get increasingly harder per round as the hand reaches deeper into your mind.

If you pass, you hold the darkness at bay for that round.

If you fail, despair seeps into your thoughts and you lose 2 STAMINA.
