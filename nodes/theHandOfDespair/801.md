You trudge onwards, the crimson sands beneath your feet endlessly rolling towards and impenetrable fortress that never gets closer. And what chance do you have against a sorcerer as powerful and cunning as Ommadon? You are nothing more than mortal? He would crush you without thinking.

Your pace slows, then slows again until with a sob you sink to your knees as the will to continue abandons you entirely. You can no longer remember why you began this journey. The quest that once burned so brightly within your heart has become nothing more than ashes.

You curl up upon the dusty red sands as the black hand looms overhead. You can go no further.

Your quest for the red crown is over.
