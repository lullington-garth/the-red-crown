You decide to stay on the outskirts of the town so nudge your horse south along Brookmeadow. The road is dusty and uneven, bordered by scrubland and pockets of wild-flowers swaying in the cold afternoon breeze. Before long you come upon a small Craftsman’s Cottage standing alone a little back from the main path. Smoke curls from its chimney and the sound of shouting carries through the air.

Outside the cottage a group of men are fighting off a large grey wolf. The beast snarls savagely, growling and baring long yellow fangs as it snaps savagely at the men. The men are armed with clubs and sticks but are still wary of the huge beast. As you near the wolf lunges, forcing the men back who fan out and try to surround the wolf.
