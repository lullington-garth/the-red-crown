The road ends at the Hellsway Inn, the last refuge before Ommadon's harsh and hostile realm.

Its weather-beaten sign creaks in the wind, hanging from rusted chains above a stout oak door. Beyond this lonely scatter of buildings lies Gormley Keep and then nothing but miles and miles of red dust dunes. A vast sea of ragged rock and sand stands between you and the Red Keep.

You try not to think about it.

For now, warm light spills from the inn's windows and the smell of woodsmoke drifts through the evening air, inviting you in.
