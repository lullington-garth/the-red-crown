Even before the last of the creatures falls to the floor, the innkeeper is at your side.

Ready for his thanks, you are surprised when he yells, red-faced with fury,

"What have you done? This is a friendly bar. I will not tolerate violence. And look... just look!"

He splutters as he gestures wildly at the wrecked furniture and blood-splattered booth.

You try to explain that they were servants of Ommadon, but the landlord waves a dismissive hand.

"Look where we are. I serve travellers from both sides of the wall. This is neutral ground! If folk start killing one another under my roof, I'll lose everything!"

With that, he marches you to the door and throws you out.

"You are not welcome here!"

The door slams shut behind you.
