The stairs wind upward behind the etched tree, each step cut rough and uneven into the stone. At the top, the passage opens into a circular turret chamber.

Shelves curve along the walls, spiralling towards a ceiling impossibly high above, all are laden with books, their spines shifting in design, shape, size and colour, flowing and reforming as you try to focus upon them. You recognise this as a library of books yet to be written. You possess one of these yourself.

One book lies open on a stone lectern. Curious as to why Ommadon would have singled out this particular volume, you lean in to read.

The words blur and reform before your eyes, unable to settle on a single telling.

" ...and as man forged a path of machine and industry, so the magical realm receded." The text shifts."The three brothers sought to forge a kingdom of magic. A realm locked away from man..."

Your heart leaps. This is the unwritten history of your current quest. No wonder Ommadon was keen to find this book. You dive deeper into the unwritten text, but it is all but unreadable.

" ...strength alone could not sustain the new kingdom ...and he settled upon reason—the laws alien but... a kingdom born of magic. Could it endure..."

The words refuse to settle, shifting and sliding across the page, each version overlapping or erasing the others. Only one line remains fixed:

"Antiquity could not choose for them..."

As you step away, the book closes itself gently.
