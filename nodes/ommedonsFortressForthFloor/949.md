The design feels ancient beyond measure and probably not carved. It feels... organics, like it somehow grew in the stone, or was always a part of them.

Three shallow pools lie spaced around the chamber, the blue etchings weaving towards and around them. Each pool is perfectly still and each has is a tiny hollow, cut into the floor at what feels like their fronts. You do not need to examine the divots to know that they are there to accept the silver acorn.
