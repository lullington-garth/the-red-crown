As the acorn settles into the stone hollow, the etchings around the pool stretch out and weave around it, encasing it in their intricate pattern.

The blue lines running across the walls pulse with a steady rhythm.

The surface of the pool shifts and you see a young man, Peter, leaning on the counter in a pawnbroker's shop.

Peter and the pawnbroker appear to be deep in discussion. Between them lies a board of a game, its pieces arranged with meticulous care.

The pawnbroker and Peter appear to be consulting the rules. The pawnbroker points to a line written in the margin. Peter nods, adjusts a piece and the game continues.

The vision fades, the etched line recede from the recess releasing the acorn.
