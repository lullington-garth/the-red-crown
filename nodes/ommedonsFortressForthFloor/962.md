Margin claps his hands together happily. "Very well! The truth, then."

He adjusts his tie and stands up straighter.

"You will be asked for a truth when you enter Ommadon's chamber," he says with uncharacteristic solemnity. "The Overseer holds the truth."

He clasps his hands behind his back and continues in a hushed, reverent tone. 

"When you reach the rooted pools... 

The first is not your friend.

The second is a strong choice for you.

And the third..." He nods soberly. "...I would avoid, if I knew what you know."

With that, he smiles brightly, his whole demeanour changing.

"There!" he says. "The truth. Exactly as promised... Biscuit?"

When you refuse, he nods and grins.

"Entirely optional, of course."

Margin settles back into his chair.

"I did enjoy that," he says as you make your way back to the landing. "Good luck with your quest."
