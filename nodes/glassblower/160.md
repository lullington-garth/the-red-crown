The tiny lady moves like she is carrying an armful of bombs. For all you know she is. She lets out a terrified squeak as one of the orbs slips free and chitters down the pile. You lunge forward, catching the falling orb as it tumbles to floor.

The woman exhales shakily. ‘Thank the stars,’ she whispers. ‘Help me, will you?’ You take several of the spheres from her arms. She visibly relaxes as you follow her to a display stand. She unloads her load, before carefully unburdening you, she then lets out a long slow breath and shakes her head.

‘I don’t know what I was thinking,’ she says, ‘I could have killed us both. I’m so sorry. If that orb had…’ she shakes her head again and grimaces. You edge away from the orbs. ‘Let me make it up to you. Let me blow a special orb for you, free of charge.’

From a nearby shelf, she produces two nuggets of glass. One burns with a deep crimson light. The other swirls with bright emerald mist.
