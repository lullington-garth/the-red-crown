You come upon a curious little shop somewhat out of place amongst the surrounding stone buildings. The entire frontage is made from thick curved panes of coloured glass that catch the sunlight in dazzling ribbons of blue, green and amber. Through the windows you can see shelves crowded with delicate glass animals, shimmering bottles and strange floating orbs.

Over the door hand a neat, painted sign: Demon Glass
