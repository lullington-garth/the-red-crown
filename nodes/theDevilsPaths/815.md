Without warning, you are swept into a screaming vortex of wind, darkness and fire, then, with a deafening, ringing howl... nothing.

You are suspended in endless blackness stretching out in all directions. Before you can scream, light explodes in front of you and three burning contracts flare into existence. You start as your hand adopts a writing stance without your willing it to, and an enormous black-feathered quill materialises between your thumb and forefinger.

A voice, somehow bigger than the infinite blackness in which you are suspended, commands:

"CHOOSE NOW... OR I SHALL CHOOSE FOR YOU."

The contracts are already beginning to disappear, consumed by the hungry flames illuminating them.

You must choose quickly.
