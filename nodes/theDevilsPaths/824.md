You descend into the fissures. The route downward is more of a tumble than a climb. You hit the bottom of the fissure hard, and it is clear there is no way back that way.

The fissure walls rise high above you. The sky appears only in narrow slits and very little light reaches these depths. With no other option open to you, you press deeper into the fissure.

The deep fissures run like the jagged branches of a lightning strike. They meander through the ragged rock in every direction, crossing each other without warning, each with no seeming identity. With no way to climb the walls and no discernible landmarks, you are lost in no time.

Panic begins to creep in. You start running at random, hitting dead end after dead end.

You curse the devil and, as if in answer, a deep rumble echoes from above.

The walls around you collapse in on themselves as the tremor brings razor rock crashing into the fissure, burying you alive.

Your quest ends here.
