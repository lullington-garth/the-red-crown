Having sorted through your possessions, choosing only those items you deem most essential, it is with a heavy heart that you abandon your horse. It has been a reliable companion and you will miss it.

You curse the devil that led you here.

"A path you chose."

You spin around.

The devil stands behind you, grinning. {scribbledMap}

Before you can reply, the devil snaps its fingers. Your horse, discarded items and the devil go up in flames. The fire burns fiercely for only a few seconds, and then all are gone.

You stand alone, surrounded on all sides by shattered plains of razor shards.
