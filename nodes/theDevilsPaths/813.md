"The safe road," the devil shrugs. "As you wish. I will not stand in your way."

It gestures to the south and, true to its word, makes no move to stop you.

It is not long before you are back to the endless vista of shimmering red sand beneath a roiling sky of murderous-looking storm clouds that never break. Red lightning rips across the heavens as the road stretches monotonously on, barren red plains followed by more, and yet more, barren red plains. Days pass in weary silence. No monsters emerge from the wasteland. No treasure presents itself. No challenge at all breaks the relentless monotony.

By the time you reach anything of note, with a numb mind you realise that you have lost all track of time. You could have been trudging for weeks for all you know...

But you are safe.
