As you leave the razor plains behind, you spot a smooth stone standing alone amidst the wasteland.

The devil sits upon it, waiting.

"Your prize," it says.

It tosses you a sturdy backpack.

"Maybe not what you were hoping for, but you will be grateful for it later."

Before you can decide whether you should thank—or curse—the devil, it is gone in a swirl of acrid-smelling smoke.

Your trial is over.
