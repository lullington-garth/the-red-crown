Darkness engulfs you. For a moment you feel weightless. The ground vanishes beneath your feet and your stomach lurches as though you are being hurled across impossible distances.

When your vision clears, you find yourself standing upon a vast plain of gleaming stone.

Countless fissures split the landscape in every direction, carving deep scars through jagged fields of razor-sharp rock.

One glance is enough to reveal a grim truth.

Your horse cannot continue.

The narrow passages between the razor rock are far too tight, and there is no safe way to bring the animal into the fissures below.

You must abandon your horse and any excess items that you can no longer carry.
