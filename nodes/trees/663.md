As you approach the towering temple doors, the silver-green light flowing along the path surges into the stone. Ancient runes shimmer softly before the great doors swing silently open.

Beyond lies a peaceful courtyard. At its centre grows an immense Silver Oak whose vast canopy fills the air with a gentle silver-green radiance. Its mighty branches spread across the entire courtyard, while roots older than kingdoms disappear deep beneath the temple itself. Every leaf glows with quiet magic, and the tree possesses a presence so ancient that it feels older than the realms themselves.

As you approach, a deep voice fills the courtyard.

While immense the voice is also warm, patient and filled with quiet strength.

"Welcome, traveller." The word ring around the courtyard. "I have watched your journey through the realms. Your quest to recover the Red Crown carries hope not only for your own people, but for every realm touched by its magic."

The leaves stir gently overhead.

"You have seen the weakness spreading through these lands. I feel it also. It reaches even into my roots."

After a thoughtful silence, the voice continues.

"I shall gift you one silver acorn... and a glimpse beyond this moment."

"You may take only a single acorn and not all my fruit are the same. The low fruit would be the logical choice, but it would take great strength to pick from the middle branches, but higher still is where the magic resides."

The glowing branches sway gently above you while you make your choice.
