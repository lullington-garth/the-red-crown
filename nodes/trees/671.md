The gatekeeper strides into the immense corridor, and you hurry after him.

Before you have gone more than a few steps, the temple shudders.

The floor lurches violently beneath your feet. Dust pours from the distant ceiling while the towering stained-glass windows rattle within their stone frames. The silver flames twist wildly as though caught in a fierce wind, throwing long shifting shadows across the corridor.

For one dreadful moment you fear the ancient windows may burst inwards. The trembling eases but does not pass.

The gatekeeper does not slow. "Quickly," he says, his voice calm but firm. "There is a great unbalance in this realm, many the wrong path trodden."

You run to keep up with the gatekeeper as the low rumbling vibrates through the passage way. 

Despite the disturbance around you, you can't help but notice that distance appears to be fluid within the passageway. Every step you take carries you impossibly far. The distant doorway is no longer distant and you marvel at the ancient and powerful magic at work in this corridor wondering of perhaps this is how the gatekeeper could be at both the beginning and end of your journey.

Within moments you stand before the great arched doorway. A vast oak tree has been carved into the ancient stone, its roots and branches covering almost the entire surface.

The gatekeeper nods towards it. "Quickly. The acorn."

You recover the acorn and press it against the carving stepping back as silver light floods through the tree, illuminating every branch before the great doors slowly swing open without a sound.
