As you approach the towering temple doors, the silver-blue light flowing along the path surges into the stone. Ancient runes flare softly before the great doors begin to swing open without a sound.

Beyond lies a tranquil courtyard. At its centre grows an immense Silver Oak. Its broad canopy shines with a radiant silver-blue light that bathes the entire courtyard in a cool celestial glow. Every branch hums with ancient magic, while roots as thick as castle walls disappear into the stone beneath your feet. The air itself seems charged with immeasurable age and wisdom.

You take only a few steps towards the tree before a deep rumbling voice fills the courtyard.

It feels impossibly old and while there is no warmth in the voice, there is no malice either. It simply speaks with the certainty of ancient wisdom.

"{playerStats.wizardName}. Your quest is essential to the continuance of magic. Not just in this realm, but every where. You must enlist a champion from the days yet to come."

The immense branches of the tree stir.

"I gift to you one of my silver acorns. Take it to the Limpid Pool within your brother's keep. There I shall await you and we will speak again to reveal what must follow."

One shining acorn detaches itself from the highest branches and falls gently into the silver grass below.

The courtyard falls silent.

You stoop to collect the silver acorn.

As you rise, you discover the gatekeeper standing quietly behind you. You had not heard him enter.

"Please," he says. "Follow me."
