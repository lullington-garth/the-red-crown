You follow the gatekeeper through the immense corridor in silence.

Distance seems to be fluid within the passage. Although your feet continue their steady rhythm across the polished floor, each step seems to carry you much farther than it should. The vast stained-glass windows drift past more quickly than seems possible, while the great doorway ahead approaches far quicker than your stride would dictate.

You glance at the silent gatekeeper walking ahead wondering if perhaps passages such as these explain how he could be at both the beginning and the end of your journey through this realm.

Within only a few minutes, you arrive before the enormous arched doorway at the other end of the passageway. Carved into its ancient stone is the image of a magnificent oak tree, its roots spreading deep beneath the threshold and its branches reaching almost to the top of the arch.

The gatekeeper turns towards you. "Touch the acorn to the door."

You press the silver acorn against the carved tree. At once, brilliant silver light spreads through every branch and root etched into the stone. The entire doorway glows silver before the great doors slowly swing open in silence.
