The gatekeeper leads you to the far side of the courtyard to an immense pair of stone doors. He raises his staff and gently touches it against the ancient stone.

A deep hum resonates through the temple. You feel a powerful and ancient magic pass between the staff and the temple as intricate silver runes awaken across surface of the door. Moments later, the doors begin to swing silently inwards.

Beyond lies a vast corridor built on a scale befitting the gatekeeper himself. The ceiling rises high overhead, disappearing into shadow, while enormous silver braziers set within carved sconces cast a cool, unwavering light the length of the passage. Between them stand towering stained-glass windows, softly backlit from beyond. They depict scenes from an age long forgotten—great figures bearing staffs, mighty temples, realms joined together in peace, and battles whose meaning has long since passed into legend.

The corridor stretches at least half a mile ahead, yet even at that distance you can clearly make out another great arched doorway waiting at its far end.
