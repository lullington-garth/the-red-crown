You reach towards the lower branches and easily pluck one of the glowing silver acorns.

The instant it leaves the tree, a vision floods your mind.

You stand before a great volcanic stone door, its surface glowing with veins of molten fire. Beyond it wait horned devils whose burning eyes never leave you. They demand of you an offering of blood.

The vision shifts and you see yourself giving your blood willingly, and while weakened by your sacrifice, you somehow know that your choice has strengthened your position rather than diminished it.

The vision fades.

You slip the silver acorn safely into your pack.

As you look up, you discover the gatekeeper standing quietly behind you. You had not heard him enter.

"Please," he says. "Follow me."
