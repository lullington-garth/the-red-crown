Calling upon your magical abilities, you reach beyond the highest branches where no mortal hand could hope to grasp. Your spell gently draws a single silver acorn free from the very top of the ancient tree.

As it leaves its branch, a vision floods your mind.

You stand before a door fashioned from opaque glass. Beyond it lies an ancient library. You see yourself reading from an enormous tome resting upon a pedestal. It seems important that you stand even though there is a comfortable reading chair, inviting you to sit and study its pages at leisure.

The great book holds magic as yet unknown to you and you feel it will be shared if you are patient.

The vision fades.

You place the silver acorn carefully into your pack.

As you turn away from the Silver Oak, you discover the gatekeeper standing quietly behind you. You had not heard him enter.

"Please," he says. "Follow me."
